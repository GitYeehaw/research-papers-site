Here, we identify a novel mechanism for high-brightness PB and demonstrate that near-ideal purity and near-ideal brightness can be simultaneously achieved in an extended nondegenerate two-photon Jaynes-Cummings model with two-body and three-body interactions. This mechanism is underpinned by a distinctive energy-level structure arising from the combined action of the two interactions.
