Here, we develop an active learning (AL) framework based on multi-objective Bayesian optimization (MOBO) to discover polymers exhibiting both high TC and low bulk modulus. Interpretability analysis further revealed molecular features associated with these trade-offs, and synthesizability assessment supported the practical relevance of the selected candidates.
